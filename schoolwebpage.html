GSCE and A-level

LRGS is known for being one of the most consistently top performing schools in the country.


More than 80% of Year 11s get grades 7-9 across all GSCE subjects they do.
More than 78% of Year 13s get grades A-A* in all A-level subjects they do.
This is very impressive considering that the average in the country is 67% for Year 11 GSCEs and 59% for Year 13 A-levels.
